Welcome to JKBK Govt. College
The roots of J.K.B.K. Govt. College can be traced back to 22nd August 1972 when the ‘New Government College’ started functioning in Ravenshaw Collegiate School, Cuttack. It was shifted on 01.01.73 to the premises of the college of Accountancy and Management Studies at Jobra and formally inauguration on 19.01.73 by the then Education Minister of Orissa Mr. Binayak Acharya.

Mission
