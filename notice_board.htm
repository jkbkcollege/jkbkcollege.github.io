1. The college calendar is an indispensable guide for the students of J.K.B.K. Govt. & Junior College, Cuttack.
2. The College Calendar is issued to a student on production of the library card.
3. The College Calendar contains the rules, regulations, necessary & relevant information about the College, the Library, different examinations, College election to the students Union and other associations, N.C.C., youth Red Cross, Proctorial Systems etc.
4. Essentially the College calendar embodies the rules for the smooth execution of various activities of the College.
5. Every student is expected to know the rules given in the College calendar.
6. Ignorance of any rule is no excuse.
